Hi I'm Tarun Kumar Jain

In grad school I fell in love with software development, so I spent the fall of 2017 at the Technosier, honing my coding skills by working on: improving my Python fluency, writing functional code, contributing to open-source projects, exploring new Fundamentals of Programming.
